## âš ï¸ **Risks & Mitigation**

### Risk: Clinical Inaccuracy
- **Mitigation:** Independent AI fact-checking + Mandatory MD/PMHNP review before publication
- **Process:** All claims verified against primary sources; Clinical reviewer sign-off documented
- **Evidence:** CLINICAL_FACT_CHECK_TMS_101.md demonstrates 98% accuracy rating

### Risk: Legal/Compliance Issues
- **Mitigation:** Medical disclaimers on every page, crisis resources prominent
- **Process:** Legal review of disclaimers and claims

### Risk: Poor User Engagement
- **Mitigation:** Analytics tracking, user feedback widgets, A/B testing
- **Process:** Monthly performance reviews, content optimization

### Risk: Content Becomes Outdated
- **Mitigation:** Review schedule (6-12 months), dated "Last Reviewed" badges
- **Process:** Assign content owners, set calendar reminders

### Risk: SEO Doesn't Perform
- **Mitigation:** Keyword research, schema markup, internal linking strategy
- **Process:** Monthly SEO audits, competitor analysis
