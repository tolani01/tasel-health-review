## **Technical Specifications**

### **File Formats**
- **Photos**: WebP primary, JPEG fallback
- **Illustrations**: SVG for scalability
- **Icons**: SVG with embedded text
- **Patterns**: SVG tileable

### **Optimization Requirements**
- **Compression**: 90% quality for photos
- **Responsive**: Multiple sizes for each image
- **Accessibility**: Alt text and descriptions
- **Performance**: Optimized for web delivery

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: Tasel Health Purple (#9D84B7)
- **Secondary**: Tasel Health Teal (#4A90E2)
- **Accent**: Tasel Health Lavender (#E6E6FA)
- **Neutral**: Medical whites and grays
- **Success**: Professional greens
- **Warning**: Soft yellows

### **Typography Integration**
- **Headings**: Professional, medical-appropriate
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable
- **Labels**: Clear, concise
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios
